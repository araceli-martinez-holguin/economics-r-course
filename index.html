Course Information

R Programming for Economists

Instructor: Araceli Martínez Holguín
Institution: School of Economics, National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM)
Semester: February–June 2026

This course introduces economics students to data analysis, visualization, and econometric modeling using R and RStudio. Students learn to manipulate economic datasets, produce visualizations, and estimate statistical models used in applied economic research.

Topics Covered

  • Introduction to R and RStudio
  • Data types and structures
  • Data manipulation with dplyr and tidyr
  • Visualization with ggplot2
  • Time series analysis
  • Linear regression models
  • Economic forecasting
  • Causal inference

Required Software

  • R (version 4.0+)
  • RStudio
install.packages(c("tidyverse","ggplot2","dplyr","forecast","lmtest"))
