About Me

I am Araceli Martínez-Holguín, a PhD student in Economics at the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM).

My research explores the relationship between extreme climate events and food prices, with a broader focus on macroeconomics, monetary policy, and sustainable finance.

I currently teach Statistical Programming with R at UNAM. This repository hosts the laboratories, materials, and examples used in the course.
